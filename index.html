ABOUT TED and TEDx


TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 100 languages.

Meanwhile, independently run TEDx events help share ideas in communities around the world A TEDx event is a local gathering where live TED-like talks and videos previously recorded at TED conferences are shared with the community. TEDx events are fully planned and coordinated independently, on a community-by-community basis. The content and design of each TEDx event is unique and developed independently, but all of them have features in common.

ABOUT THEME


AAROHA – a word that arouses multiple emotions in one’s heart. For students, it’s the optimism to achieve their dream, for musicians it’s the expression of going from an octave of tranquility to an even higher octave of exuberance, for others it’s the ascend of people towards a new revolution. Looking further, Aaroha usually couples with Avaroha. It’s the melody signifying the ups and downs of life. A gush of emotions flow through one; now you are brimming with cheerfulness, the next moment you may feel sorrow. Yet we always cherish and hunt for the happiness. With an attempt to introduce you to a crew of conquerors who have paved the path for the world to ascent, the theme AAROHA connotes that with the greatest strength of mind and the slightest ray of hope, anyone can climb the steepest of the hills. Aaroha is not merely a word, instead it is a concept that narrates the tale of an acorn growing into an oak despite the numerous atrocities. Introspecting over the word, you will find a deeper meaning hidden in your experience, your thoughts, and your beliefs. This stimulating and insightful edition inspires us to gather all our might and fly higher fulfilling our dreams.
